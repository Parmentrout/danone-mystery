Locked Out! A Danone Mystery...

You were working on a new food formula late last night and when you left you accidently reset the company's security code. Now everyone has been locked out of the office! It's up to you to figure out the new code so everyone can get back in! Luckily, your orchestrator and part-time wizard Jen Pease will send you weekly clues to escape. Each week will unlock one part of the code and when they are all open you can get back in!

You have unlocked your office!!
